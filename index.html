Ananya Renuka Balakrishna

Postdoc in Materials Engineering

Massachusetts Institute of Technology



I am a postdoc in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at MIT.  I develop and apply mathematical models to investigate the link between microstructures and material properties in materials. I have been supported by the Lindemann Postdoctoral Fellowship, and received my PhD in Solid Mechanics and Materials Engineering at Oxford, supported by the Felix Scholarship.
