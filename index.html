Ananya Renuka Balakrishna

Postdoctoral Researcher

University of Minnesota


I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Aerospace Engineering and Mechanics Department at the University of Minnesota. I develop and apply physics-based models to investigate the links between material microstructures and material behavior. I completed my PhD in Solid Mechanics and Materials Engineering at the University of Oxford as a Felix Scholar, before pursuing postdoctoral research at MIT as a Lindemann Postdoctoral fellow.
