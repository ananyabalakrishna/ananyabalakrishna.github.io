Mathematical Design of Energy-related Materials

Today, we have a need to not only discover materials with enhanced properties but to do them urgently – there is a need to establish a quantitative design framework to accelerate materials development. Finding these design principles is the focus of our research group. In this talk, we share our recent findings on magnetic materials and battery materials, and propose design conditions to enhance their performance and lifespans.
